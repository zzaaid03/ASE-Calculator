## Communication And Accessibility

- WhatsApp is the main platform for quick updates and discussions.
- Team members respond within 12 hours unless they inform the group in advance.
- Important project decisions are shared with the entire group.

## Meetings And Collaboration

- The team meets once per week online or on campus.
- Additional meetings are scheduled before important deadlines when needed.
- Members who cannot attend inform the group and review meeting updates afterward.

## Code And Quality

- GitHub is used for version control and separate branches are used for changes.
- A task is complete only when it runs correctly, is tested, and is pushed successfully.
- Team members review code changes before merging them into `main`.

## Problems And Conflicts

- Members ask for help early when blocked.
- Missed deadlines and contribution concerns are discussed openly.
- Repeated lack of participation is addressed as a group and reported when necessary.
